Store Hours

Generally, Jimmy’s locations are open from:


  • Monday–Friday 7AM–6PM
  • Saturday–Sunday 8AM–6PM

With the following exceptions:


  • Gerrard Sat-Sun: 8AM-5PM
  • McCaul Sat-Sun: 8AM-5PM
  • Kensington Sat-Sun: 8AM-7PM
  • Queen West Mon-Sun: 7:30AM-6PM

Holiday Hours

Jimmy’s locations are usually open on every holiday except Christmas Day.
For the latest holiday hours, check our Instagram or view each store’s in-depth info on Google Maps.

For Here or To Go

All drinks are currently served in takeout cups, but you’re welcome to enjoy them in-store at any of our locations. Get 25¢ off your drink if you bring your own mug!

Payment Types

Jimmy’s is cashless, but we accept all major debit and credit cards.

Pet Policy

You're welcome to use your laptop for as long as you would like.
Seating at our locations is first-come, first-serve. Wi-Fi is available at all of our locations.

Refunds and Exchanges

Well-behaved pets are welcome at these locations:

  • Kensington Market (191 Baldwin Street)
  • Royal York/Mimico (380 Royal York Road)

For other locations, please check with staff for guidelines.

Accessibility

We strive to accommodate all customers! Here’s a quick overview of our location accessibility features:

  • 191 Baldwin St – Free Wi-Fi | Indoor & Outdoor Seating | Takeout | Wheelchair Accessible
  • 84 Gerrard St W – Free Wi-Fi | Indoor Seating | Takeout
  • 166 McCaul St – Free Wi-Fi | Indoor Seating | Takeout | Wheelchair Accessible
  • 107 Portland St – Free Wi-Fi | Indoor & Outdoor Seating | Takeout
  • 15 Ossington Ave – Free Wi-Fi | Indoor & Outdoor Seating | Takeout | Wheelchair Accessible
