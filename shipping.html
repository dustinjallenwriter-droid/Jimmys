Physical Gift Cards

You can pick up physical gift cards at any one of our locations or buy a digital one through the Gift Cards section of our website.
We accept all forms of our gift cards in-store and online.

E-Gift Cards

You can send digital gift cards to individuals or groups of people.

You can purchase e-gift cards online and send them to yourself or the recipient via text or email. We offer various designs that you can choose for your special occasion and you can choose when they receive the gift card in their mailbox.

To use your e-gift card in-store, you just need to have your gift card number ready to read out to the barista!

Shipping and Delivery

Currently, we only offer shipping within Canada. Once we’ve received your order, it takes about 2–5 business days for us to get it ready for shipping.
The carrier takes an average of 7 business days to deliver, depending on distance and delays.
If you haven’t received your order after 14 days, please contact us and let us help you sort it out.
