What’s Included

Do you have an event coming up?
We offer coffee boxes that can serve 10-12 people!

Choose from our full-bodied medium roast or bold dark roast. Check out our menu to learn more about our Bean Options.

Each coffee box comes equipped with:

  • Cups, lids, and stir sticks
  • Cream, milk, or dairy alternative
  • Sugar and/or alternative sweetener

How to Order

Feel free to place an order with us in-store, by phone, or through email.
Email responses take 2–3 business days, so for quicker service or same-day pickups, it’s best to call or stop by in person.
You can choose to pay in-store at the time of ordering, or when you pick up your order if you’ve placed it by email or phone.
Please provide the name and phone number of the person who will be picking it up, as well as your expected pick-up time when placing your order.

Processing Time

The earlier we receive your order, the better! In some cases, we can put together a coffee box for you in about 15–20 minutes. This depends on the time of day and how busy the location is.

If you’re in the downtown area, feel free to call ahead to other locations to see if they might have an opportunity to fulfill your needs.
